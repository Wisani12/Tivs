Company Overview

Timbavati Medical Services (TMS) is a South African-based Occupational Health, Wellness and Occupational Health & Safety company with extensive expertise in Occupational Medicine, Occupational Hygiene, Employee Wellness and Primary Healthcare.

TMS is a proudly 100% black-owned company providing comprehensive Occupational Medicine & Health, Occupational Hygiene, Occupational Therapy, Occupational Health & Safety, Wellness and Primary Healthcare services to clients within both the public and commercial sectors.

Established in 2010, the organisation was founded with the objective of improving health and safety standards within workplaces across diverse industries.

TMS collaborates closely with occupational hygiene specialists, dieticians and occupational therapists to deliver integrated workplace health solutions.

These partnerships ensure high-quality services delivered in compliance with South African legislation and international best practices in occupational health and safety.

Director Profile

Dr Courage Tiyiselani Mkhawana

MBChB • Occupational Medicine Practitioner

Dr Mkhawana is a registered Independent Medical Practitioner with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) with extensive experience in Emergency Medicine, General Practice, HIV Management, Occupational Medicine and Infectious Disease Management.

He previously served as a Medical Officer at Dr Yusuf Daddo Hospital in Gauteng where he managed and treated COVID-19 patients during the peak of the pandemic. He also worked as a Resident Medical Officer at Netcare Pinehaven Hospital managing ICU and COVID wards.

Dr Mkhawana manages and operates three travel clinics in Gauteng and is licensed to administer Yellow Fever vaccinations and other infectious disease certifications.

He obtained his Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBChB) in 2006 and later completed postgraduate qualifications in Occupational Medicine & Health and Tropical Medicine & Health.

His interests include preventative medicine, workplace healthcare and construction safety. He also organizes the annual Soweto Wellness Race promoting healthy lifestyle awareness.
